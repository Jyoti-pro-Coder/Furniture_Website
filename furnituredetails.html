FAQ

Q. How To Arrange A Sofa Set In Living Room?

Place the sofa in such a way that you can watch the TV from it comfortably. It is also necessary to place it close to any other seating furniture you have to ensure conversations can happen easily. Be mindful to leave gaps so that movement is not restricted.

Q. How To Clean Sofa Set?

Vacuuming works on all sofas and is one of the easiest ways to remove dust and other dirt from them. Cleaning stains, food spillage, etc., depends on the material of your sofa’s upholstery. Leather sofas might do with a simple wet cloth and wiping down, whereas fabric sofas may require cleaning solutions.

Q. Which Sofa Set Is Best: Leather Or Fabric Or Wooden?

Wooden sofa sets are durable because the frame can last a long time. If the cushions/cushion covers wear out, you can easily replace them or change covers. Leather sofas are durable and stylish, but they are less comfortable; not to mention, expensive. Fabric sofas are available in a range of options, and are not too expensive, although a bit difficult to maintain. Buy one that suits your needs and budget.

Q. What Should I Keep In Mind Before Buying A Sofa?

The most important is the size of your space. Also, do some research beforehand about the kind of material you want, what might look good with the rest of your decor, and your budget.
